Getting started is easy!

We break down introductory content into a self-paced eight (8) modules. This is one module per week of about 2 hours per day of content (10 hours per module) leaving you weekends to review and hone your learning. Of course no collection of content can deliver you everything you could want, which is why we have curated some links with each module where you can further deep-dive into a topic that interests you.

At the end of this Masterclass you will have transacted Bitcoin Cash. Minted your own token. Created paper wallet and split keys. You will have time-locked a trasaction and will have interacted with a script. We hope you love what we have prepared for you, and please send us your feedback with any part of the course your feel lacks clarity or where we have oppotunity to improve. We strive to make the best content possible.
